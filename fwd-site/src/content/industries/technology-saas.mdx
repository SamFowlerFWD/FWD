## Building Products That Ship

Speed matters in tech. Whether you're validating an idea with an MVP or building v2 of an existing product, you need a developer who understands the difference between a polished prototype and a production-ready application, and knows when each one is the right call.

I built Ask Stephen from concept to App Store, a full coaching platform with native iOS apps, habit tracking, video streaming, and subscription billing. That project taught me what it takes to ship a real product, not just write code but handle the entire lifecycle from architecture to deployment.

## What I Build for Tech Companies

**MVPs and prototypes** that let you test your idea with real users before committing to a full build. I focus on the core value proposition first and add features based on what users actually want.

**User dashboards** that make your data useful. Clean interfaces, real-time updates, and the metrics that actually matter to your users. Not just a wall of charts nobody reads.

**API integrations** that connect your product to the tools your customers already use. Whether that's Stripe for payments, Twilio for messaging, or custom webhooks for automation.

**Landing pages** that convert. Not a template with your logo swapped in, but something built around your specific product and audience. Fast-loading, mobile-friendly, and designed to turn visitors into signups.

## Why Work With Me

I'm a developer who builds products, not just features. I understand the startup mindset because I've been there. I co-founded Ask Stephen and took it from idea to paying customers. That means I think about user experience, business model, and technical architecture together, not in isolation.
