## The Booking and Membership Problem

Gyms, studios, salons, and therapists all share the same frustration: too many tools doing too little. You've got one app for bookings, another for payments, a spreadsheet for memberships, and a notebook for the class timetable. Nothing talks to anything else, and you spend more time managing systems than managing your business.

I've worked with businesses like Salon 64 in Dereham and Millers Fitness to sort out exactly this kind of mess. The goal is always the same: bring everything into one place, automate the repetitive stuff, and give you back your evenings.

## What We Build for Health and Fitness Businesses

For gyms and studios, we build class booking systems where members can see the timetable, reserve a spot, and get reminded before their session. If a class is full, they join a waitlist and get notified automatically if a space opens up. No more managing this by hand.

Membership management is another big one. We create systems where members sign up, pay monthly by direct debit or card, and manage their own account. You get a dashboard showing who's active, who's overdue, and who's cancelled. Renewals happen automatically, and you only step in when something needs attention.

For salons and therapists, the focus is usually on appointment booking with online payment. Customers pick a service, choose a time, and pay a deposit or the full amount upfront. Automated reminders go out before the appointment, which dramatically reduces no-shows.

## Reviews and Reputation

One thing health and fitness businesses often miss is collecting reviews systematically. We set up automated follow-up messages after appointments that ask for a Google review. It's a small thing that makes a big difference to how you appear in local search results.

Whether you run a personal training studio, a hair salon, a physiotherapy practice, or a yoga class, the technology should work around your schedule. We build systems that do exactly that, nothing more complicated than it needs to be.
